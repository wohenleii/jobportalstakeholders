JOBPORTAL — FULL STORYBOARD

Full-Stack Job Portal System  |  Node.js + Express + MySQL + Bootstrap  |  Apr 2026 – Jul 2026

1
HOME PAGE
Step 1
localhost:5000
▸User visits localhost:5000
▸Hero section with search bar
▸Live stats: jobs, users, companies
▸Featured job cards displayed
▸Browse by category
▸Clicks Sign Up to register
Entry Point & Job Discovery
2
REGISTER ACCOUNT
Step 2
localhost:5000/register.html
▸Select role: Student or Employer
▸Enter name, email, password
▸Password hashed with bcrypt
▸Account saved to MySQL DB
▸JWT token issued on success
▸Redirected to home page
Role-Based Account Creation
3
LOGIN
Step 3
localhost:5000/login.html
▸Enter email & password
▸Demo buttons for quick auto-fill
▸bcrypt compares hashed password
▸JWT token stored in localStorage
▸Admin → redirected to dashboard
▸Student → redirected to home
Secure Login & Role-Based Redirect
4
BROWSE JOBS
Step 4
localhost:5000/jobs.html
▸Search by keyword
▸Filter: type, category, location, salary
▸Paginated results from API
▸Job cards with badges and salary
▸Click title → detail modal
▸🔖 Bookmark icon on each card
Real-Time Job Search & Filter
5
JOB DETAIL & APPLY
Step 5
jobs.html → modal
▸Modal shows full job info
▸Salary, deadline, company details
▸View tracks via job_views table
▸Click Apply Now → cover letter
▸Application saved to DB
▸Status starts as Pending
Job View Tracking & Application Submit
6
BOOKMARK JOBS
Step 6
jobs.html → bookmark
▸Click 🔖 on any job card
▸Bookmark saved to bookmarks table
▸Icon turns yellow when active
▸Click again to remove bookmark
▸Toast notification shown
▸View all bookmarks in Profile
Save Jobs for Later
7
MY PROFILE
Step 7
localhost:5000/profile.html
▸Edit name, bio, skills, resume URL
▸Tab: My Applications
▸Status: Pending → Shortlisted → Hired
▸Tab: Bookmarked Jobs
▸Remove bookmarks from here
▸Profile saved via PUT /api/auth/profile
Track Applications & Manage Bookmarks
8
EMPLOYER: POST JOB
Step 8
localhost:5000/post-job.html
▸Employer logs in with JWT
▸Fill title, type, category, location
▸Set salary range and deadline
▸Write description & requirements
▸POST /api/jobs → saved to DB
▸Job live on Browse Jobs instantly
Employer Job Submission Form
9
ADMIN DASHBOARD
Step 9
localhost:5000/admin.html
▸Login redirects admin to dashboard
▸Stats: Users, Jobs, Applications, Views
▸Chart.js: jobs by category & type
▸Top 5 most viewed jobs table
▸Sidebar navigation with sections
▸GET /api/admin/stats
Real-Time Platform Overview
10
ADMIN: MANAGE USERS
Step 10
admin.html → Users
▸Click Users in sidebar
▸Filter by role: Student / Employer / Admin
▸Table: ID, Name, Email, Role, Joined
▸Click 🗑️ to delete user
▸DELETE /api/admin/users/:id
▸Cannot delete own admin account
User Management & Role Control
11
ADMIN: MANAGE JOBS
Step 11
admin.html → Jobs
▸Click Jobs in sidebar
▸Filter by status: Active / Closed / Pending
▸Inline status dropdown per job
▸PUT /api/admin/jobs/:id/status
▸Delete jobs with 🗑️ button
▸Shows views count per job
Job Approval & Status Control
12
ANALYTICS & REPORTS
Step 12
admin.html → Analytics
▸Click Analytics in sidebar
▸Line chart: job views last 30 days
▸Line chart: applications last 30 days
▸Table: most applied jobs ranking
▸Data from job_views table
▸GET /api/admin/analytics
Job View & Application Analytics
